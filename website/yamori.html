Overview概要

What is YAMORIYAMORIとは

A Chrome extension wallet for the Midnight blockchain. Biometric login, seedless creation, in-browser ZK proofs, and the most complete DUST management in the ecosystem.Midnightブロックチェーンのための Chrome拡張機能ウォレット。生体認証ログイン、シードレス作成、ブラウザ内ZK証明、エコシステム最も完全なDUST管理。

🤚

Biometric Seedless Wallet生体認証シードレスウォレット

Fingerprint or Face ID creates your wallet instantly. No 24-word seed phrase. WebAuthn PRF derives keys in your device's secure enclave.

🛡

ZK Ownership Proof

Prove you own a wallet without revealing your keys. Zero-knowledge proof using Poseidon hashing and JubJub curve cryptography.

⭐

DUST Generation DashboardDUST生成ダッシュボード

Live DUST balance, generation progress, manual registration, and Cardano cNIGHT bridge status. All in one view.

⚡

In-Browser ZK Proofsブラウザ内ZK証明

Custom Zig WASM prover at 133KB generates proofs locally. No Docker, no servers, no trusted third parties.

🔐

SHA-512 + PBKDF2

Full BIP39 derivation chain implemented in Zig. SHA-512 hashing and PBKDF2 key stretching compiled to WASM — no JS crypto dependencies.

📊

NightForge Explorer ToolsNightForgeエクスプローラーツール

10 interactive tools: DUST Calculator, Privacy Score, Validator Dashboard, Contract Observatory, Network Health, and more.

🔗

DApp ConnectorDAppコネクター

Connect to Midnight DApps via the window.midnight.yamori API. Transfer, sign, and track transactions from any connected application.

📱

Multi-Platform Biometricsマルチプラットフォーム生体認証

Mac Touch ID, Windows Hello, iPhone Face ID, and Android fingerprint. One wallet creation flow across every platform.
